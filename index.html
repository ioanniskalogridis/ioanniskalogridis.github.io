Ioannis Kalogridis

Welcome to my webpage! I am a Lecturer in Statistics and Data Analytics at the School of Mathematics and Statistics of the University of Glasgow.

I work on functional data analysis, nonparametrics, and robust statistics. I obtained my PhD in 2020 from the Department of Mathematics, KU Leuven, Belgium under the supervision of Prof. Stefan Van Aelst.

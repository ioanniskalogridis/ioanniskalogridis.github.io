Ioannis Kalogridis

Welcome to my webpage! I am a Lecturer in Statistics and Data Analytics at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Glasgow.

I work on functional data analysis, nonparametrics, and robust statistics. I obtained my PhD in 2020 from KU Leuven under the supervision of Prof. Stefan Van Aelst.
